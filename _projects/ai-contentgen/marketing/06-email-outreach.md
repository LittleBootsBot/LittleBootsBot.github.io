## Outreach Checklist

### Target Categories (aim for 100+ total)
- [ ] 20 Python/dev tool YouTubers
- [ ] 15 developer newsletter authors
- [ ] 15 dev tool Twitter/X accounts (10k+ followers)
- [ ] 10 Python podcast hosts
- [ ] 10 tech bloggers who cover CLI/productivity tools
- [ ] 10 Reddit power users in r/Python, r/DevTools
- [ ] 10 Dev.to/Hashnode top authors in Python/AI tags
- [ ] 5 developer advocates at relevant companies (Anthropic, GitHub, etc.)
- [ ] 5 tech journalists covering developer tools

### Outreach Rules
1. **Personalize every email.** No mail merge without a real first sentence about their work.
2. **One ask per email.** Try it, write about it, or give feedback. Not all three.
3. **Follow up once.** After 5 days. Then stop.
4. **Track everything.** Spreadsheet: name, email, date sent, template used, response, outcome.
5. **Offer value first.** Generate a sample README for their project before emailing.
6. **Accept "no" gracefully.** A polite decline is better than being marked spam.
7. **Time it right.** Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM in their timezone.

### Email Metrics to Track
- Open rate (target: 40%+)
- Reply rate (target: 15%+)
- Positive reply rate (target: 8%+)
- Coverage secured rate (target: 3%+)
